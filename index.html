About Me

Hey there! My name is Eddy Bobadilla (Eduardo technically if you want to be formal). I'm a former filmmaker and videographer venturing into the world of web development. My journey into coding is a bit unconventional as I went to college, Portland State University, for film production. After graduating with my Bachelor of Arts, I began a wedding and event videography business. I did pretty well and then 2020 happened. With business drying up I decided to re-evaluate my career and knew I had an interest in web development having learned basic HTML and CSS, as well as UI design from a few graphic design classes I took in high school and college. 18 months of learning later, here I am! Although the journey has been challenging, I've enjoyed every step of the way and am eager to add more tools to the toolkit to become the best developer possible.
